I am a 3rd-year M.S. student at Tsinghua University, where I am fortunate to be advised Prof. Hairong Lv, focusing on the research of machine learning and computer vision.

I am currently work as an intern at CCVL, Johns Hopkins University, where I am honored to be supervised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan L. Yuille.

My research interests span in the fields of visual representation, contrastive learning, and machine learning theories. My current research concentrates on self-spuervised pretraining of convolution-based and transformer-based visual representation models.
